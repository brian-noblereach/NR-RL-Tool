    Welcome to the Readiness Level Assessment Tool

    This tool helps research teams, early-stage ventures, and their advisors assess commercialization readiness across 8 key dimensions.

    Use it to measure progress for pre-incorporated research projects or very early-stage ventures—tracking how de-risked an idea becomes as it hits key milestones.

    How to Use

    • Select a category from the sidebar to begin assessment
    • Choose a readiness level that best describes the venture's current state
    • Levels are cumulative—selecting Level 5 means Levels 1-4 have been achieved
    • Review your summary in the panel at bottom-right
    • Export or save your assessment at any time

    For Advisors

    Readiness levels provide a structured framework, but every venture is unique. Use your professional judgment when circumstances don't perfectly match a level definition. The goal is to capture a meaningful snapshot of where the venture stands today—not to force-fit into rigid criteria.

    Deliverables and indicators are suggestions, not requirements. A venture may demonstrate readiness through equivalent evidence.
